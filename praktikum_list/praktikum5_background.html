Praktikum 5 - Background

Background body menggunakan background-color: #b0c4de;

background-color: #ff0000 (HEX)
background-color: rgb(0, 128, 0) (RGB)
background-color: orange (Nama Warna)
background-image: linear-gradient(...)

Properti Background lainnya:

  • background-image: url("paper.gif")
  • background-repeat: repeat-x | repeat-y | no-repeat
  • background-attachment: scroll | fixed
  • background-position: center | left top | right bottom
