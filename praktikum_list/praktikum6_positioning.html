Praktikum 6 - Positioning

1. Static (default)

position: static

2. Relative (geser dari posisi normal)

position: relative; top: 30px; left: 50px

3. Absolute (di dalam container relative)

position: absolute; top: 20; right: 20

4. Fixed (tetap saat di-scroll)

position: fixed
